Super Smash Brothers Brawl

A small portion of Brawl's cast posing:

Super Smash Bros. Brawl is a 2008 entry to the Super Smash Brothers series by Nintendo. Smash Brothers is a crossover fighting game franchise where characters from different series can duke it out in a 2d platform fighter. Super Smash Bros. Brawl is the Wii’s entry to the series releasing 2 years after the Wii. Brawl included 39 different playable characters ranging from Nintendos most iconic characters like Mario, Pikachu, Link, and Kirby to third party characters like Snake from the Metal Gear franchise. Super Smash Bros. Brawl 2 most known modes are the adventure mode: Subspace Emissary and the 1v1 ruleset. Subspace Emissary is known as the Smash Brother’s franchise’s best adventure mode as it has a full in depth story along with unique boss fights that would not return to future Smash Brothers games. Super Smash Bros. Brawl’s 1v1s were very popular and competitive, escalating to the point where tournaments such as MLG Dallas 2010 which had a 1 st place prize of $35,000. Super Smash Bros. Brawl is in the style of a 2d fighter where the character you are playing as can move along the X and Y axis, every character in the game had 2 jumps or more allowing everyone in the game vertical mobility. Every character also has a unique set of attacks such as Up Specials, which would help your character recover from getting knocked off of the stage and also Down Airs which would generally send anyone trying to recover back down. Super Smash Bros. Brawl sold over 15 million copies and was the predecessor of Super Smash Brothers U otherwise known as Smash 4.
